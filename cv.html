Professional Artist Statement

Ari Zed is a reality creation artist specializing in consciousness-expanding visionary paintings. With over two decades of dedicated practice, his work bridges mystical experience and contemporary artistic expression, creating what he terms "psychic landscapes" designed to seduce viewers into expanded states of awareness.

Based in Hamilton Hill, Western Australia, Ari's practice integrates active imagination techniques, oil painting mastery, and philosophical inquiry to create artworks that function as both aesthetic experiences and consciousness technologies. His vision extends toward establishing temple-communities where art becomes a vehicle for collective consciousness evolution.
