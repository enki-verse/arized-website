The Journey Begins

From the Victorian bush of Australia emerged a vision that would challenge the very nature of reality itself. What started as world-building through fantasy roleplaying evolved into something far more profound—a rejection of the conventional suburban path that society calls "normal" but I recognized as being "asleep at the wheel."

Through two decades of dedicated practice, I've mastered the visionary techniques of active imagination and mystical experience, developing what I call "psychic landscape painting"—artworks that don't just depict reality, but actively seduce viewers into expanded states of consciousness.

The Magician's Castle

My work is grounded in the understanding that consciousness is primary—the material world is merely its extrinsic appearance. Through art, I create bridges between mind and matter, helping others construct their own "reality tunnels" that maximize personal freedom and authentic expression.

We are not here to escape the body, but to embody mind into understanding. Each painting becomes a doorway, each brushstroke a conscious act of reality creation.

Artistic Practice

My paintings explore the liminal spaces where consciousness meets form—figurative works that blend sci-fi mysticism with deep psychological insight. Themes of transformation, eroticism, apocalypse, and consciousness expansion weave through canvases that range from intimate 50cm pieces to epic 200cm+ works designed to completely transform the viewer's perceptual field.

Working primarily in oils, I create what I call "psychic landscapes"—paintings that function as visual technologies for consciousness expansion. Each piece is designed not just to be viewed, but to be experienced as a gateway into expanded states of awareness.

The Fertile Ascent

My mission extends beyond individual artworks toward something revolutionary: the creation of temple-communities where consciousness evolution becomes a lived reality. This is "The Fertile Ascent"—a movement toward post-rational consciousness through the integration of art, philosophy, and community.

Currently based in Hamilton Hill, Western Australia, I'm working toward establishing a creative sanctuary where visionaries and consciousness explorers can gather, create, and push the boundaries of what it means to be human in the 21st century.
